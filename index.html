Empowering Youth Through AI and Technology

AI in STEM is a student-centered nonprofit organization dedicated to providing youth STEM learners worldwide with the knowledge, experience, and community they need to thrive in our rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Join a vibrant network of student innovators, researchers, and changemakers who are shaping the future of artificial intelligence and technology. Together, we're building tomorrow's solutions today.
