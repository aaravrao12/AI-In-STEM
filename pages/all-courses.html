Our Curriculum

Our curriculum offers a wide variety of courses designed to spark curiosity and develop the passions of STEM-driven students across diverse interests!

Intro to Machine Learning

Learn the fundamentals of machine learning algorithms, data preprocessing, and model evaluation techniques to build intelligent systems.

Supervised, Unsupervised, & Reinforcement Learning
Introduction of Machine Learning Models
Model Evaluation & Validation
Dataset Preparation

Intro to Neural Networks

Intermediate • 2 Lessons

Understand how neural networks make predictions and learn through training to solve complex pattern recognition problems.

Neural Network Layers
Training Neural Networks
Tuning a Model
Convolutional & Recurrent Neural Networks

Intro to NLP & LLMs

Beginner • 1 Lesson

Explore natural language processing and large language models to understand how computers process human language.

Natural Language Processing
Large Language Models (LLMs)
Transformer Architectures
Text Preprocessing & Tokenization

Beginner • 1 Lesson

Discover how computers interpret visual data and perform object detection, segmentation, and image classification tasks.

Object Detection
Object Segmentation & Labeling
Using YOLO
NIDS-NET Segmentation Pipeline

Intermediate • 1 Lesson

Dive into mechatronics systems that combine intelligent algorithms with mechanical and electrical engineering principles.

Adaptive AI Control
Sensor Fusion
Vision in Mechatronics

AI Applications in Python

TensorFlow

Climate Modeling
