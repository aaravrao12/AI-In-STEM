About AI in STEM

Empowering the next generation of AI innovators through accessible education!

Our Mission

AI in STEM is a student-centered nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering youth STEM learners worldwide with the knowledge, experience, and community they need to thrive in the evolving world of technology.

We strive to accomplish this by offering detailed yet student-friendly educational content, including slidedecks, videos, and articles, covering a wide range of topics revolving around artificial intelligence, machine learning, robotics, etc.

Using these materials, AI in STEM chapters partner with local schools and community organizations in cities across the US to host free workshops and educational meetings for STEM-interested youth. These workshops are led by passionate student volunteers dedicated to making a positive impact in their community.

Our Values

Our values guide everything we do, from the programs we create to the communities we serve. They represent our commitment to making AI education accessible, impactful, and transformative.

Accessibility & Inclusivity

We are committed to making AI education available to all, breaking down barriers of cost, location, and prior knowledge. We champion diversity and ensure our programs are welcoming and beneficial to students from every walk of life.

Empowerment & Innovation

We empower students to become creators and critical thinkers, not just consumers of technology. We foster an environment that encourages experimentation, problem-solving, and the development of innovative solutions to real-world challenges.

Community & Collaboration

We believe in the power of collective learning and mutual support. We cultivate a vibrant, global community where students, educators, and professionals can connect, share knowledge, and collaborate on projects that drive positive change.

Excellence & Impact

We are dedicated to delivering educational content and experiences of the highest quality. Our programs are designed to have a tangible impact on students' lives, equipping them with practical skills and inspiring them to pursue impactful careers in STEM.

Ethical AI & Responsibility

We instill in our students a strong understanding of the ethical implications of AI and the importance of responsible technology development. We encourage them to consider the broader societal impact of their innovations and to use AI as a force for good.

Continuous Learning & Adaptability

In a field as dynamic as AI, continuous learning is paramount. We foster a mindset of lifelong learning and adaptability, preparing students to navigate and contribute to an ever-changing technological landscape.
