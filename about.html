About AI in STEM

What We Do

AI in STEM is a student-centered nonprofit organization with the mission of providing youth STEM learners across the nation with the knowledge and experience they need to thrive in an increasingly AI & technology centered world.

We strive to accomplish this by offering detailed yet student-friendly educational content, including slidedecks, videos, and articles, all focused on various topics revolving around artificial intelligence, machine learning and robotics.

Using these materials, AI in STEM chapters then partner with local schools and community organizations in cities across the US to host free workshops and educational meetings for STEM-interested youth. These workshops are led by passionate student volunteers dedicated to making a positive impact in their community.
