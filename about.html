About AI in STEM

Empowering the next generation of AI innovators through accessible education!

Our Mission

AI in STEM is a student-centered nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering youth STEM learners worldwide with the knowledge, experience, and community they need to thrive in the evolving world of technology.

We strive to accomplish this by offering detailed yet student-friendly educational content, including slidedecks, videos, and articles, covering a wide range of topics revolving around artificial intelligence, machine learning, robotics, etc.

Using these materials, AI in STEM chapters partner with local schools and community organizations in cities across the US to host free workshops and educational meetings for STEM-interested youth. These workshops are led by passionate student volunteers dedicated to making a positive impact in their community.

Our Values

Our values guide everything we do, from the programs we create to the communities we serve. They represent our commitment to making AI education accessible, impactful, and transformative.

Accessibility & Inclusivity

We are committed to making AI education available to all, breaking down barriers of cost, location, and prior knowledge. We ensure our programs are welcoming, inclusive, and beneficial to students worldwide.

Empowerment & Innovation

By empowering students to become creators and critical thinkers, we foster an environment that encourages experimentation, problem-solving, and the development of innovative solutions to real-world challenges. Through this, we're promoting the next generation of STEM innovators!

Community & Collaboration

Our organization is designed to maximize the power of collaborative learning and mutual support. We cultivate a vibrant, global community where students and mentors can connect, share knowledge, and empower each other.

Excellence & Impact

We are dedicated to delivering educational curriculum and other resources of the highest quality while making them as accessible as possible. Our programs are designed to have a tangible impact on students' lives, fostering their passion for STEM and inspiring them to pursue impactful STEM careers!

Leadership & Mentorship

AI in STEM's primary goal is to uplift students like us from areas all around the world. Leadership and mentorship are quintessential in this process, allowing us to work with and guide students through courses in STEM and technology.

Continuous Learning & Adaptability

In a field as dynamic as AI, continuous learning is paramount. We foster a mindset of lifelong learning and adaptability, preparing students to navigate and contribute to an ever-expanding technological landscape.
